Stock Trend Prediction

This web application uses a Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) model to predict the stock price trend for the next 30 days based on the previous 100 days of historical stock data. By analyzing past price movements and patterns, the LSTM model forecasts whether the stock is likely to experience an upward (bullish) or downward (bearish) trend in the coming month. This allows investors to get an idea of short-term market movements.These predictions provide insights into the near-term behavior of the stock, helping investors make more informed decisions about their investments.
