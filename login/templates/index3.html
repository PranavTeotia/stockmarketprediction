News Sentiment Analysis

This web application uses the FinBERT model to analyze financial news articles and determine their sentiment. The model processes news headlines and reports to classify them as either Positive, Neutral, or Negative based on the content. The sentiment score indicates how strong the sentiment is in the news article, with a higher positive score representing more positive sentiment and a higher negative score indicating more negative sentiment. These scores help investors quickly gauge the tone of the latest news and its potential impact on stock performance.
